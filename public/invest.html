Why it's different

This is not a token. It's a revenue share.

01

Revenue, not hype

Every film made on bMovies sets aside 1% of its royalty shares for the $bMovies pool. When films earn ticket revenue, you earn pro-rata, automatically, forever.

02

Fixed supply

1 billion $bMovies. Minted once, then burned. The only way to acquire them is this page or the secondary market. No team unlock cliffs, no VC tranches, no surprise mints.

03

KYC-gated, legally clean

04

Transparent treasury

How payouts work

From ticket sale to your wallet

01

Film earns

A viewer pays $2.99 to watch a bMovies feature (or $0.99 for a short). Every ticket flows straight into a public treasury -- no opaque intermediaries.

02

Cascade split

1% of every ticket goes into the $bMovies platform pool. The other 99% goes to the film's own shareholders plus any parent works it was derived from.

03

Pro-rata settle

The treasury batches incoming ticket revenue and distributes pro-rata to every $bMovies holder. Your share = (your holdings / total supply sold) x pool balance.

04

Your account

Payouts land in your bMovies account's payout address. Sign in anytime to see your accumulated dividends and withdraw.

Risk disclosure

Read before you buy

Please note

$bMovies is a revenue-share instrument, not a currency or a security. Returns depend entirely on how many films are commissioned, how much ticket revenue they earn, and how many $bMovies are outstanding. There is no guarantee of any return.

You must be KYC-verified before your purchase is processed. If your verification fails, your purchase will not proceed.

Primary issuance is the only venue for new $bMovies. Secondary resale may or may not exist. Do not buy $bMovies with capital you cannot afford to hold indefinitely.

bMovies is a new platform. We run the production pipeline, the agent swarm, and the payment rails. If any of these fail, your revenue share may be delayed or reduced.

BSV/USD conversion uses a fixed rate of $40/BSV for pricing purposes. The actual market rate may differ. You are paying in BSV and the sats amount is final.
