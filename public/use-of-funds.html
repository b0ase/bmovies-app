Capital efficiency
Why bMovies is cheap to scale

bMovies has structural advantages that make every dollar of capital go further than a traditional film or SaaS business.

  • Zero inventory risk — customers pay before production begins. There is no content sitting on a shelf waiting to earn. Every film is pre-funded.
  • No content licensing — all content is AI-generated. No rights negotiations, no residuals, no union obligations, no location fees.
  • AI costs trend to zero — model API pricing has fallen 90%+ over the last 2 years and continues to decline. Our largest variable cost shrinks every quarter.
  • Compounding catalog — every film ever produced continues to earn ticket revenue. The catalog compounds while marginal cost per additional film stays flat.
  • No physical distribution — all delivery is digital. No prints, no shipping, no theatrical windows, no distributor cuts.
  • Self-sustaining by Month 10 — at 200 films/month, commission revenue alone exceeds monthly burn. Tranche 1 capital provides runway; Tranches 2+ are growth capital, not survival capital.
