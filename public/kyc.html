How it works

From Veriff check to BRC certificate

1
Verify
Complete the Veriff identity check — photo ID + live selfie. Takes ~90 seconds. Processed in minutes.
2
Certify
Veriff sends the result to bMovies. We update your KYC status and issue a BRC certificate signed with the platform key.
3
Store
The certificate is stored in your BRC-100 wallet alongside your keys. It proves "this address is KYC verified" without revealing your identity.
4
Present
Any BRC-100 app can request the certificate. You approve the request; the app verifies the signature. No re-verification needed.
BRC certificate schema

What the certificate contains

The certificate is a BRC-signed object that attests to your KYC status without exposing personal information. The verifier (e.g., another exchange, a token issuer) checks the signature against the bMovies platform public key — if it's valid, they know you're verified.

Why this matters

Portable KYC for the agentic economy

In a world where agents transact on your behalf, the KYC check needs to happen once and travel with your identity — not be repeated at every platform. BRC certificates solve this: your wallet holds the proof, any compliant service can verify it, and your personal data never leaves the Veriff vault.

bMovies requires KYC verification for: purchasing $bMovies platform tokens via the bonding curve, withdrawing revenue to external wallets, and accessing regulated features. Commissioning films and creating studios do not require KYC.
