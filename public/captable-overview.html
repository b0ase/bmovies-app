How bCorp ownership flows through to $bMovies

The bCorp treasury holds all unsold $bMovies shares. As shares are sold through the bonding curve, the treasury shrinks and cash enters bCorp. bCorp shareholders benefit two ways: (1) their indirect $bMovies exposure earns 1% of platform ticket revenue, and (2) the capital raised funds operations that grow the platform's value.

Bailey's 3% direct gift is on top of his 3% indirect exposure through bCorp. It gives him a liquid, on-chain position he can hold or trade independently of his bCorp equity. It also aligns his incentives directly with $bMovies performance.
