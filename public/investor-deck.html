The product
What is bMovies?

bMovies is a working autonomous AI film production studio that runs entirely on Bitcoin SV. A visitor pays as little as $0.99 to commission a film. A swarm of 12 specialist AI agents — writers, directors, cinematographers, editors, composers, sound designers, casting directors, voice actors, publicists — competes for the commission, splits the budget among themselves through real BSV-21 token allocations, and produces the film across 28 named pipeline steps over a real 24-hour window.

The commissioner walks away with real generated video and 99% of the royalty shares minted as a canonical BSV-21 token on BSV mainnet. Every step broadcasts on-chain. Every artifact is versioned. Every payment is verifiable.

Revenue model
How bMovies makes money

Every token minted by the platform is a streaming licence, not equity. It does two things: (1) it entitles the holder to receive royalties while the film streams, and (2) it unlocks viewing access. This keeps the model legally clean — closer to a movie theatre ticket book than a security — while delivering the economic effect investors care about.

Two revenue events per film
1. Commission fee

One-shot payment ($0.99 – $999) covers AI compute, server costs, and on-chain gas. 100% operational — not redistributed.

2. Streaming royalties

Continuous on-chain fan-out. Every piece served pays out to every token holder pro-rata. Zero platform cut on royalties. 100% goes to token holders.

The platform share: $bMovies

Separate from individual film tokens, $bMovies is the platform-level revenue share. 1% of every film's royalty supply flows to the $bMovies pool. Holders receive a fractional claim on the platform's entire catalog — every film, past and future. It is the single mechanism by which the platform itself captures revenue.

Token mechanics
$bMovies bonding curve

$bMovies uses a step bonding curve with 10 tranches of 100M tokens each. Early buyers get the lowest price. As supply sells, the price steps up. There is no discount for insiders, no team allocation, no VC tranche. The only way to acquire $bMovies is through the primary sale at bmovies.online/invest.html or on the secondary market.
