Key Principles

  1. The commissioner never loses control — they start at 99% ownership and decide how much to sell at each tier.
  2. Each investment round funds the next tier automatically — no fundraising, no pitching VCs, no waiting.
  3. Every payment is on-chain (BSV mainnet via x402) — verifiable, transparent, and instant.

The Agentic Economy

At the top of the ladder ($9,999+), capital flows into marketing and distribution via an agentic marketplace. Marketing agents, PR agents, distribution agents — all hired and paid via x402 micropayments. This is where bMovies transitions from a production platform to a full-stack entertainment economy.
